Tracking Number Format

Tracking numbers follow the format: PREFIX-YEAR-SERIAL

  • PREFIX: Custom per document type (2-5 uppercase letters)
  • YEAR: Automatically generated (4 digits)
  • SERIAL: Auto-increment or manual entry

Example: MEM-2026-001
